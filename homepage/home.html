Meet Spatial Virtual World

Step into an immersive digital experience with Spatial.io. Explore the Aapravasi Ghat like never before—walk through its historical corridors, interact with virtual exhibits, and uncover the rich history of Mauritius in a 3D environment.

With Spatial.io, we bring the heritage of Aapravasi Ghat to your fingertips, offering a seamless blend of culture and technology. Experience the past, present, and future all in one place.

Key Features:

  • Interactive Exploration: Navigate through detailed 3D models of Aapravasi Ghat.
  • Virtual Artifacts: View and learn about historical artifacts with a simple click.
  • Accessible Anywhere: Enjoy this journey from the comfort of your home on desktop, mobile, or VR.
  • Connect with Others: Chat, collaborate, and share experiences with other users in real time.
